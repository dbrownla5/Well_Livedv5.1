## 01 — HOME ORGANIZATION & MODERN MOVE
*Your home, made to work for how you actually live.*

For the room that keeps collecting piles, the move that technically happened but never settled, the closet that no longer fits your life, and the spaces where everyday friction quietly steals time. The work flexes to the level of support that feels right for your day, your energy, and the decisions the room requires.

**Ways we can work together**
- **Side-by-side guided sessions** — the glued-at-the-hip version: sorting in real time, building systems together, testing what works for your habits, learning to maintain the reset, coaching through the emotions of change. Any style of change that creates a positive outcome is the right style for the client.
- **Hybrid decision sessions** — I do the heavy lift first (sort, categorize, clear visual overwhelm, stage donation/resale/hold/family piles, create manageable groups). Then you step in for the part that needs your judgment (yes / no / why / what needs more time / what becomes resale / what moves next). The room becomes manageable first, then the decisions become obvious.
- **Full key handoff** — for repeat clients, House Call continuity clients, retainers, longer-trust relationships. Trust-earned access (door codes, keys, concierge desks, multi-day access) that lets the project keep moving while your life does too.
- **Move-in landing support** — for relocations and modern moves, the room is built around your arrival timeline. You land, the bed is made, chargers work, coffee can be made, the closet accepts clothing, and tomorrow morning already works.

**Included in every session:** planning, layout logic, sourcing recommendations, label systems, setup and install, pre-order coordination, and all daily workflow tools/utility supplies — cord wraps, Velcro, painter's tape, reusable sorting bins, cable adapters, garment steamer, tape, measuring tools, install basics, problem-solving hardware, and the random adapters/fasteners that always become necessary. One quality rolling rack travels in the car as part of the active workflow kit (closet edits, move days, landing sessions, keep/donate/resale staging, overflow clothing, steaming first-week workwear); if it's rotating at handoff quality and the room genuinely needs one, I'll suggest leaving it as part of the final system.

**Move-in & first-week landing bundles** *(fixed but flexible — the mix shifts to the room's most immediate needs; budget and expectation stay clear):*
- **$150 — Essentials Landing.** First apartment, guest room, nursery overflow, short-term landing, stressed-mom reset, "just make it easy" move. Charging extensions, starter bins, hanger refresh, hamper, one guest-facing bin, towel/kitchen starters, trash bags/paper towels/soap/TP.
- **$250 — Start-Up Landing.** Move-ins, professionals landing in LA, family-room transitions, work-zone startup, one-room fast reset. Stronger closet logic, towels, starter dish set (2 plates / 2 bowls / silverware), paper towels, trash flow, better charging, visible bins, shelf dividers, bathroom utility, work-zone startup.
- **$500 — Full Landing Bundle.** New-city arrivals, life transitions, furnished-apartment resets, baby prep, post-breakup move-ins, temporary homes that still need to feel adult. The *sleep, shower, eat, charge, and get dressed tonight* bundle: towels, dish set, paper goods, soap, bins, dividers, charging, hangers, hamper, flat lamp bases, Wi-Fi bulbs, mirror, closet startup. Items move around depending on what makes the first 72 hours easiest.

**Product planning for deeper organization:** for closets, storage rooms, offices, linen closets — product support is planned *after* the walkthrough (shelf depth, hidden overflow, duplicate volume, drawer dimensions, what stays visible, guest-facing polish, whether the answer is bins/dividers/hangers/trays or no product at all). Realistic expected buy range presented before we begin. **Clarity before commitment** — if hidden volume changes the smartest path once we're in the room, I show the updated strategy before it's a spend decision.

**Communication + decision rhythm:** we define what needs your yes, what can move independently, how check-ins happen (text, photos, FaceTime, weekly recaps, custom forms), and how much interruption your day can hold. Goal: the smallest possible change to how you already communicate.

**Intake + first call:** a light intake form so I arrive prepared (first room, what keeps fighting the space, what must work tonight, logistics, optional prep photos), then a call already grounded in the real work.

**PRICING:** $150/hr · 3hr minimum · landing bundles at $150 / $250 / $500 · custom product planning quoted after walkthrough.

## 02 — LEGACY PLANNING & INVENTORY CATALOG
*The operational layer of what quietly adds up inside a home.*

For gracefully aging households, adult children supporting remotely, inherited storage, downsizing homes, younger professionals managing inherited volume, and anyone overwhelmed by sentimental accumulation. Lives in the operational middle: the drawers, the backup bedding, the vintage with story, the everyday things with future value, and the objects that deserve clarity while someone is still living in the home.

**What you receive (possible outputs):** digital inventory, photo documentation, room-by-room item notes, family distribution references, sentiment/story preservation, resale-ready routing notes, donation pathways, second-home/storage references, transition roadmap, printable copies, bound family binders, duplicate family copies, digital/web-based workflows.

**Floor plan reference (when included):** a simple reference layout for orientation and continuity — not architectural work. Shows where things live, how the home functions, where overflow builds, how future family can understand the house quickly.

**Story + provenance (for one-of-a-kind and vintage):** ownership story, era, maker, family memory, local history, design significance, collector relevance. Anything identified for resale routes directly into Curated Resale & Consignment without restarting the process.

**PRICING:** single room or storage unit $175/hr · 2hr minimum · whole home quoted after walkthrough.

*For the things life leaves unfinished.*

For people who suddenly find themselves handling the parts of home life someone else used to quietly carry. The appliance install. The login. The donation run. The gift mountain after the baby shower. The guest room before family arrives. The 4-hour practical reset that makes life livable again.

**Ways to use it:** one-time visits, weekly support, monthly continuity, seasonal resets, remote family check-ins, post-project upkeep, retainer continuity, "I need a 4-hour you."

**Vendor + project oversight (when needed):** movers, haulers, cleaners, installers, contractors, storage teams, shipping/courier, resale buyer coordination, donation pickups, service-appointment access. Los Angeles is the core; wider travel by project quote.

**PRICING:** $175/hr · 2hr minimum · retainer continuity available for repeat clients.

## 04 — CURATED RESALE & CONSIGNMENT
*For things that still have value, story, or second-market potential — without asking you to become a part-time reseller.*

Best for clothing, designer accessories, jewelry, home decor, furniture, art, rare textiles, story-rich vintage, one-of-a-kind pieces with real buyer demand.

**Easy handoff (whatever works for your week):** city pickup, route-based stops, Uber Business courier, building desk drop, UPS Access Point, direct bag shipment, handoff during another project block.

**Intake standards:** all intake bags are new, clean, and selected for safe transport; generally not reused between clients (hygiene + client protection). Confirm items are clean, dry, pocket-checked, compartment-checked, ready for release into temporary care and custody.

**Where items sell (routed by market fit):** Poshmark, eBay, Etsy, Facebook Marketplace, Chairish, Vestiaire, Grailed, vetted local resale, private collector network, future hosted client sales.

**Intake review (7–10 business days):** confirmation of what arrived, what's accepted, what may be rerouted, what may need hold timing, what may not justify resale.

**Sale timing:** most strong items sell in the first 90 days. Standard consignment term 180 days unless otherwise requested. Rhythm: 30 days review · 60 days pricing adjustment · 90 days route review · 180 days completion · optional extension. *Resale rewards patience, not urgency.*

**PRICING:** commission-based · agreement required.
