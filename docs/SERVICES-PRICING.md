## Company positioning
The Well Lived Citizen is an **operational lifestyle support company**: home organization, move support, continuity systems, practical life management, legacy inventory, curated resale, asynchronous operational support, transition management, staged closeouts, household continuity. Core positioning: **operational relief.** Built on trust-based workflows, flexible support, route-based efficiency, asynchronous continuity, and relationship-based service.

The pricing separates into three commercial tiers: **Entry services** (low-friction conversion) · **Core services** (primary hourly/project work) · **Continuity services** (long-term retention).

For: organization · unpacking · move support · room resets · closets · kitchens · systems · continuity · layout optimization · setup · household functionality · move staging.
- **Flex Blocks (prepaid):** 10-Hour **$1,250 prepaid** · 25-Hour **$3,150 prepaid**. For ongoing/phased work, move transitions, continuity, larger resets. **Hours do not expire.**

### Home Closeouts & Staged Transitions — **$150/hr** (+ resale commission for any items sold)
For when a client must leave before the home is fully closed out. Continued operational handling after relocation: packing final items, labeling, separating storage/resale/shipping categories, donation routing, temporary storage logistics, listing approved resale items, phased shipping, UPS drop-offs, final clean exits, closing out temporary units. Useful for cross-state moves, downsizing, inherited homes, divorce transitions, delayed housing timelines, furnished temporary living. *This is staged operational closeout support — not indefinite storage management.* The hourly pays for **managing the transition**; resale commission pays for **long-tail resale execution** (separate operational systems). **Client covers:** storage unit · shipping labels/postage · movers · courier costs · specialty hauling · third-party logistics.

For: storage rooms · inherited objects · antiques · home records · warranty documentation · downsizing · fragile item routing · inventory creation · preservation systems · resale routing · appraisal coordination.

May include room-by-room cataloging, photo documentation, appraisal coordination, preservation routing, resale integration, continuity systems, archival organization.
- **Continuity Support — $175/hr:** inventory updates, inherited item integration, routing, storage updates, ongoing preservation.

### Curated Resale + Consignment — **commission-based · agreement required**
**Intake:** no booking fee, no pickup fee. Approved methods: route pickup · personal pickup · Uber courier · approved shipping label · UPS Access Point. All intake begins through an approved intake request; only approved bags/containers/methods accepted unless authorized.

**Commission (on net sale price, after platform fees, shipping, approved transaction costs):**

| Category | Client | TWLC |
|---|---|---|
| Clothing & Accessories | 45% | 55% |
| Designer & Luxury | 50% | 50% |
| Furniture & Significant Home | 50% | 50% |
| Full Closet Liquidation | 45% | 55% |

**Low-value volume adjustment:** bags primarily of lower-value items may require adjusted commission due to labor-to-value ratio — reviewed at intake, communicated before listing.

**Fast Bag Pickup workflow:** complimentary bags generally provided in 60 qt / 90 qt depending on size/category/volume. Smaller pickups may require approved bag formats or be coordinated during existing route stops, scheduled pickups, discounted Uber courier, low-cost UPS labels, or UPS Access Point transfers. Goal: keep operational costs low before earnings.

**Item preparation authority:** may steam, lint roll, polish, spot treat, perform minor repairs/light sewing, stabilize vintage, coordinate cleaning when justified — reasonable prep when it meaningfully improves resale outcome. **Second-life condition:** items are pre-owned/estate/vintage and may show wear consistent with category; good-faith fairness expected from buyers.

**Furniture & home goods:** a major part of the work; overlaps with resets, downsizing, moves, legacy. May include resale management, buyer coordination, photography, staging guidance, marketplace routing, pricing, listing prep, pickup logistics, and temporary operational holding during active transitions (not indefinite storage). Large staging/mover coordination may shift into project pricing.

**Furniture / item research:** single item, one stop, one focused question ≈ **$25** (light research, quick review, drive time, communication). Multi-item / collections / estate-level → House Calls, Resets, or Legacy scope. Not certified appraisal/authentication unless separately arranged.

**180-day resale review:** most strong inventory sells in ~6–9 months; standard term 180 days. At ~180 days, unsold items may be continued, rerouted, returned, donated, or handled per client preference. Reasonable attempts to contact client before rerouting/disposal.

## ⚙️ INTERNAL OPERATIONAL GUIDANCE — NOT PUBLIC
*Pricing gravity, not rigid rules — for consistency under pressure, quoting, and onboarding help. Clients never see this.*
- **Travel:** most LA absorbed; outskirts/outside zone → reasonable fee OR project-integrated; multi-state → scoped, travel folded in.
- **Furniture / large goods:** standard 50/50; large staging/mover coordination → project support; temporary storage selective/limited, no indefinite holding.
- **Small research:** one item/one question → complimentary OR ~$25; multi-item → House Call / Reset / Legacy scope.

- **Emergency / rush:** same-day prioritized when possible; rush courier/special logistics → pass-through costs possible.
- **Walkthrough:** simple projects folded in; larger projects walkthrough-informs-scope; legacy is walkthrough-first.
- **Async work:** hourly/project may include routing, coordination, resale prep, communication, inventory, scheduling, documentation, continuity — not only in-home hours.
